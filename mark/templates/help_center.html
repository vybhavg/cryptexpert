Account Setup

Learn how to create and secure your CryptExpert account.

  1. Register with your email and create a strong password

  3. Set up two-factor authentication for extra security
  4. Customize your profile settings

Wallet Management

Connect and manage your exchange wallets securely.

  1. Navigate to Wallet Management section
  2. Select your exchange (Binance, CoinDCX, WazirX)
  3. Enter your API keys (read-only recommended)
  4. View your balances and transaction history
  5. Set up automatic refresh intervals

AI Price Predictor

Get AI-powered cryptocurrency price predictions.

  1. Select a cryptocurrency pair (e.g., BTC/USDT)
  2. Choose your prediction timeframe
  3. View historical price analysis
  4. Get future price predictions with confidence intervals
  5. Save or export your predictions
